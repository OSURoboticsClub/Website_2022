The OSU Robotics Club (OSURC) is one of the largest student-run clubs at Oregon State University, comprising of nearly 200 members! Each year, the club runs multiple project teams such as Aerial, Drone Racing, Mars Rover, Underwater, and VEX U to help our members become better engineers through practical real-world experiences. Additionally, OSURC provides a stocked workspace in Merryfield Hall (Room 103), complete with computer workstations, soldering stations, electrical components, 3D printers, and many other tools that are available for use by any of our members.

Robotics Club graduates have gone on to work at a range of leading tech companies such as SpaceX, Honeybee Robotics, Tektronix, NVIDIA, Intel, and NASA JPL.
