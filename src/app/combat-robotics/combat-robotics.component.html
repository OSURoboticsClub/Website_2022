Combat Robotics

2023-2024 Meetings: Fridays 1pm-5pm in Merryfield

Combat Robotics is a newer team from the Combat Robotics Club centered on building and competing with lightweight robots. Members of Combat Robotics focuses on the one-pound Plastic Antweight division, and with a larger team the twelve-pound Hobbyweight class.

For more information, contact the Team Osian Leahy at leahyo@oregonstate.edu or the main Combat Robotics website at https://clubs.oregonstate.edu/combat-robotics.
