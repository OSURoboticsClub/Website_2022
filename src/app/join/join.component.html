Membership

Memberships last until the beginning of fall term next school year. Membership will give you access to all of the club's resources, which includes computers, electronics, hand tools, and a ton of other useful stuff for building robots!

Mailing Lists

If you are or want to become a member, join osurcmembers@engr.orst.edu. Only officers are authorized to post to this list.

If you have a question about the club or want to get information distributed to the club, send an email to osurcofficers@engr.orst.edu.
